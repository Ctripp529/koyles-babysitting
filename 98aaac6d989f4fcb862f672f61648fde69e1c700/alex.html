Alex Sleep Time

Alex usually naps about every 2-3 hours after waking up.

Once a day he will have a longer stretch of awake time, 3-4 hours

I will let you know if he is going to have a long stretch with you.

Naps can last up to 2+ hours, you don't need to wake him.

Fresh diaper and Magic sleepsuit for sleeping. Sometimes he will take a binky, but usually he spits it out.

Alex Play Time

Alex is actively rolling over on to his stomach and sometimes rolling back over. Please watch where you set him down, he will roll off!

Alex likes to "talk" and will coo back and forth with you. If you mimick his noises you can get him to smile or laugh.

There are two playmates(one upstairs and downstairs), he enjoys looking and interacting with the toys attached.

If you want to go outside with him, please sit in the shade or use the pop up tent for shade.
