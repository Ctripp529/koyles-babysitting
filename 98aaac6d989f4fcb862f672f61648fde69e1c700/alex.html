How to make a Bottle

Alex Sleep Time

Alex is dropping down to one nap a day, usually he has a morning nap that is about 3 hours and a short nap lasting 20-40 minutes in the afternoon. But sometimes he will be up later and not take the morning nap consolidating into one long nap in the middle of day.

This means he has some days a longer stretch of awake time, 3-4 hours

I will let you know if he is going to have a long stretch with you.

Alex Play Time

Alex is actively crawling EVERYWHERE and will stand himself up againslt anything. Please watch where you set him down, he will crawl away!

Alex likes to "talk" and will coo back and forth with you. If you mimick his noises you can get him to smile or laugh.

The walker or jumper upstairs are big hits. The tent downstairs is a great containment option for him. Though Olive knows how to undo the zippers.

If you want to take him outside in the cooler weather make sure he is bundled- head, hands, & toes!

You can find hats, gloves, jackets, shoes, and anything to go out in the front hall closet.
